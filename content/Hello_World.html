Let's start with something familiar:

If you've ever written your first line of code, you probably recognize the line above. It's the traditional starting point — the digital equivalent of introducing yourself to the terminal and saying, "Hey, I'm here!"

Today, I'm doing the same — not in a terminal, but right here in this blog. After many lines of HTML, CSS, JavaScript (and yes, plenty of coffee), this blog is finally live!

This space is my corner of the internet where I’ll share thoughts, projects, bugs, breakthroughs, and brain dumps — especially around coding, software development, AI, and data science. Whether it’s diving into a tutorial or reflecting on a weird bug I fought for hours, it’ll all live here.

So to anyone reading: welcome! Whether you're new to code or a seasoned dev, I hope this blog brings something useful, fun, or just relatable.

Just like every program starts with a "Hello, World!", every journey begins with a first step. This is mine — and I can't wait to see where it goes.
